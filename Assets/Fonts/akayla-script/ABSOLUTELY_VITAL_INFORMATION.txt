Akayla Script is a beautiful, genuine calligraphy typeface. 
It is swashy, yet crafty, which contributes to giving any project a personal touch. 
With multiple stylistic and swash alternates, this font is subject to great variety. 

The family comes in a regular style, and with a bonus Deco style with more swashy and decorative letters. 

It contains ligatures, and has support for all Latin based European languages.
